Colour blindness test

Welcome on a Colour blindness test site. Here you can check whether or not you have difficulties with colour differentiation. Click “Do a test” to proceed.

How does it work?

Here we use the Ishihara colour test planes to determine your ability to recognize colours. You will see 15 different colour planes. Each plain contains one or two numbers, that have different colour than the background. The example of such plane is shown below. You need to select what number do you see from answers listed below each picture and click “Next” to proceed to the next plane. At the end statistics are calculated, where you can see the time you needed to answer each question and how well did you performed.
